Roast Veggies and Sausage

Description

Roast veggies and sausage is a perfect low effort comfort food. It's healthy and filling and never disappointing. Everyone in the family will be begging to have more!

This recipe has plenty of room for customization to your taste. Nearly any veggie you can think of goes great in the mix and spice can be adjusted to your taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 package pre-cooked andouille sausage
  • 1 pound red potatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1/2 head cauliflower
  • 2 medium turnips
  • 12 oz grape tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Chop veggies into approximately 1 inch chunks
  3. Cut sausage into approximately 1/4 to 1/2 inch discs
  4. In a large mixing bowl, toss the chopped veggies with the oil and seasonings until evenly coated
  5. Spread the veggies in a single layer on a baking sheet and place in the oven. Bake for 15 minutes
  6. Remove the tray from the oven and add the tomatoes and sausage, spreading evenly among the veggies
  7. Return the tray to the oven and bake 20 more minutes or until veggies are tender and sausage has golden brown edges
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool 5 minutes before serving.
